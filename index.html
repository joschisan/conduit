Bitcoin Community Custody

Fedimint lets communities create private community banks on Bitcoin called Federations. Conduit is your wallet to access them and make Lightning, Bitcoin and internal eCash payments all from a single app.

What is Fedimint?

Fedimint is a federated Chaumian e-cash mint backed by Bitcoin. It allows communities to collaboratively custody Bitcoin while providing users with strong privacy guarantees through blind signatures. Multiple guardians share custody of the funds, eliminating single points of failure.

Learn more at fedimint.org
